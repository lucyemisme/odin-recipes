How To Process Your Pumpkin

  1. Find or grow a Cheese Pumpkin
  2. Set oven to 350 degrees
  3. Cut the pumpkin into large parts
  4. Remove abnd put aside seeds and strings
  5. Lay the parts with the meat side facing up in a large baking pan
  6. Cook until a fork easily slides through the meat. Usually about 40 minutes but ovens and pumpkins vary
  7. Remove from the oven and allow to cool just enough to allow you to hold the pieces
  8. Scrape the meat out of the skin into a bowl
  9. Discard the skin parts (Find local chickens who will love this)
  10. Run the meat through a food processor or blender immersion blender until smooth
  11. Divide up the pumpkin puree into 1 or 2 cup amounts. Save what you will use soon in the refrigerator and freeze what you will use later
  12. Add to your favorite pumpkin recipe!
