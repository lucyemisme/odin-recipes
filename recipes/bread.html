Pumpkin Bread

Description

Pumpkin bread is our family favorite recipe. I always use my own pumpkin puree from a Cheese pumpkin I grew or purchased. In our opinion there is no better pumpkin. The pumpkin taste is more mild and lends itself perfectly to this sweet bread. To see how to process a fresh pumpkin click here

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 beaten eggs
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 3 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 teas baking soda
  • 3/4 teas salt
  • 1 teas cinnamon
  • 1 teas nutmeg

How To Prepare

  • Grease really well 2 bread loaf pans or a large sheet cake pan
  • Set oven to 350 degrees
  • Mix all the ingredients in the standard way - all dry together and then all wet together and then mix both parts together
  • Pour into the greased pan(s)
  • Bake about an hour for the thick loaf pans and about 40 minutes for the sheet cake pan
  • You want a knife inserted into the middle to come out clean. Ovens and pans are all different so keep a close eye on it the first time you make it
